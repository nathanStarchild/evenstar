General

To celebrate my 40th Birthday, I want to gather together all of the communities that I pour so much time, love and energy into. Camping at a beautiful Central Coast location, surrounded by nature, interactive art, friends, music and creativity.

Dates: 16th - 18th August 2024

Theme: Out of this world

Leave No Trace: This is a leave no trace event. No bins are provided. Pack in, pack out.

No Chemicals in the Creek: None whatsoever. No shampoo, soap, detergent, sunscreen, insect repellent etc even natural ones.

Drive Slow: All attendees are asked to drive slowly down the gravel road coming in and out of the property - there are many walkers, cyclists, horse riders, kids and wildlife on the road.

Ammenities: There is a big beautiful toilet block with compost toilets and (hot) showers. TP and hand soap provided. Please read the signs explaining how the compost toilets work.

Water: All water on the property is unfiltered rainwater and/or creek water. Please bring your own drinking water. There are two taps / wash up stations in the main paddock. Please be mindful and keep showers short.

BYO and DIY: There will be a big pot luck dinner on Friday Night, and some bits and bobs here and there on Sat, but other than that please bring your own food, cooking set up, plate, bowl, cup, cuttlery, Bring your drum we will have a big drum around the fire on friday night. Bring your instruments for open mic and jams. Bring your flow toys, LED and fire especially.
